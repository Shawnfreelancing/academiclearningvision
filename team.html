History & Our Mission

We started with a dream to help faculty implement a standard for the online course component of a face-to-face course to improve the probability of student/faculty/institutional success.

We set a standard that leverages existing institutional technology to create environments where both students and faculty thrive.

By expanding the learning sweet spot and removing tech-related barriers, we help more students succeed.

Our goal is to define the new gold standard for the online component of a face-to-face course.

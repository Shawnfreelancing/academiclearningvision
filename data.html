Student Survey Findings Summary

This is only using 47 responses and is not indicative of how our info will turn out.

Key Findings:

  • Strong Support for Technology in Learning
    • Over 80% agree or strongly agree that technology helps them learn outside the classroom.
    • 78% support a quality standard for online course components.
    • 87% believe faculty should receive training to integrate technology effectively.
  • Desire for 24/7 Interactive Learning
    • Over 80% agree or strongly agree that a 24/7 learning component with interactive features makes in-person time more productive.
  • Mixed Perceptions of Professors' Current Use of Tech
    • Many agree professors are comfortable using tech, but fewer agree it is used effectively or interactively.
  • Low Awareness of Bloom's Taxonomy
    • Fewer than 20% understand Bloom's Taxonomy well.

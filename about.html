History & Our People

We started with a dream to help faculty implement a standard for the online component of face-to-face courses to improve the probability of student, faculty, and institutional success.

Our goal is to leverage existing institutional technology to enhance outcomes. By expanding the learning sweet spot and removing barriers to engagement, we create a clearer path to success.

We define the new gold standard for integrating technology into face-to-face learning to support every learner and educator.
